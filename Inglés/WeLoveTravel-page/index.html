Guadalajara

Metropolitan Cathedral

It’s the heart of the historic down town of Guadalajara. It’s construction began around 1560 and it took more than 50 years to finish it. Discover the architecture and beauty of its interiors.

Degollado Theater

Neo classical architecture in Guadalajara It’s the actual venue of the Philharmonic Orchestra of Jalisco. It has a capacity for 1,015 people. Visit and discover the beauty and history of this architectonic monument during our tour.

Minerva

It's a beautiful monument to Minerva, roman goddess of wisdom, arts and war; it’s the equivalent to the Greek Atenea. Built in 1950s as the opening of the México-Morelia-Guadalajara freeway, celebrating the 400 years foundation of the ¨Tapatio Pearl¨. Walk this Guadalajara attraction during our tour.

Tequila

Distillery

Its old ranches and distilleries produce the famous and emblematic distilled beverage of Tequila, which is much more than just a spirit, it is an entire culture full of history from its tequila ranche

Tequila Express

If you want to make your visit to tequila country a memorable day-long excursion, take the tequila train.

Volcano of Tequila

Extends its arms to embrace this colorful "Magical Town". It is a land of blue-gold that rises from the foot of the Río Grande Canyon and the landscape of agave crops.

Local Food

The gastronomy of Jalisco is one of the richest and most varied in the country. This state offers tourist tours that share its history through its 500 years of miscegenation, contributing to the fact that Mexican cuisine is declared an intangible heritage of humanity by UNESCO due to its complex elaboration processes and ingredients typical of the region.
